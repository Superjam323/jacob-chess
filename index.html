About me

Hi, I'm Jacob

I've been playing chess since kindergarten and competing seriously ever since — most recently placing top 10 in nationals. I love helping others discover the game, whether you're just learning how the pieces move or trying to level up your strategy. I'll meet you where you are.

My lessons focus on understanding the "why" behind every move — not just memorizing openings. We'll work on tactics, strategy, and how to think like a chess player.
